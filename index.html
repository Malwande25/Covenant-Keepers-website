Our Story

Covenant Keepers was founded in 1998 through the vision of Burning Bush Ministries.

The organization was established with the singular purpose of developing the whole man.

It serves men of all ages, nationalities, and backgrounds.

True to its name, Covenant Keepers is guided by the Word of God, with the conviction that solutions to life’s challenges are found in Christ and His Word.

In 2019, Covenant Keepers was formally registered as a Nonprofit Organization under registration number 228-225 NPO.
