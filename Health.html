Health & Wellness

Vision

To promote health consciousness in families by delivering preventative and

corrective awareness programs for individual and community well-being.

Description

Promoting physical fitness, mental resilience, preventative healthcare awareness, and holistic well-being.

Activities include:

  • Monthly health awareness webinars focusing on men’s health
  • Mental health awareness workshops
  • Nutrition and lifestyle education
  • Community fitness programs Weight Shredder Competition
