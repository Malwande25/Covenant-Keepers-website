Vision

To foster a society where every child has an involved, responsible, and committed

father, thereby strengthening families and communities.

Description

Equipping men to lead their homes spiritually and practically, nurturing strong families and future generations.

Key initiatives:

  • Fatherhood mentorship programs
  • Development of a Fatherhood Book
  • Family bonding events and retreats (e.g Annual Father & Son Gala Dinner, now inclusive of daughters)
  • Community “Imbizo” gatherings, encouraging men to openly discuss challenges without stigma
