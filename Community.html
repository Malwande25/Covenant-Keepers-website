Vision

To restore moral fiber by winning men through Christ-centered initiatives.

Description

This portfolio addresses pressing social issues such as gender-based violence, crime, drug and

alcohol abuse, and deaths during initiation practices. It collaborates with police, schools, and

NGOs, while also offering workshops on behavior, communication, circumcision, and

marriage preparation.
