Our Story

Covenant Keepers is the men’s department of Burning Bush Ministries, birthed through prayer to build men who influence church and society.

The name reflects a man who understands his covenant with God through Jesus Christ.

This covenant defines identity, purpose, and responsibility.

The department serves men of all ages and backgrounds.
