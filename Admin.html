Secretary General – Office of Administrators

Vision

To serve as the vital link between leadership, members, and external stakeholders by

enabling strategic leadership, efficient administration, and effective communication.

Description

Overseeing governance, record-keeping, compliance, departmental coordination, and strategic administration.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collecting, presenting, and archiving organizational work across regions.
  • Managing departments of Sales & Marketing, Events Management, Monitoring & Evaluation, and Administration.
  • Overseeing day-to-day operations, ensuring functionality across all portfolios.
  • Producing quarterly, semester, and annual reports.
